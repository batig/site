Happy April Fools' Day

Well, obviously nothing of this ever happened. :)

The Venue is magnificent as you ‘ll witness yourself firsthand when you join us in Athens. Everything is going as planned.

We still have lots of work to do before we’re ready to welcome you to Athens this June for EuRuKo 2013, however we are getting there. Our CFP is open and a lot of proposals have been submitted already. We suggest you get a look at them and vote for those you like and make suggestions to improve the ones you feel need a more thorough description. And why not read the process and submit your own proposal?

Really soon, we will announce the first batch of tickets.

Stay tuned and follow us closely on Twitter, our blog feed and our mailing list.
